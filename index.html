About Me

I have comprehensive experience in computational biology through both research and teaching roles. Previously, I worked as a Lecturer in Biology and Specialist Technician & Research Assistant at the University of Essex.


My research interests are targeting steroid receptor signalling in diagnosing and treating cancer disease, and the application of data science and machine learning in cancer research.


I hold a PhD in Molecular Medicine and a Postgraduate Certificate in Higher Education Practice (PG CHEP) from the University of Essex.
